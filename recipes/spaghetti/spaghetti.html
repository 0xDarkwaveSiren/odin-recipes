Golden Strings

Description

A timeless Italian favorite, this spaghetti recipe features perfectly cooked pasta tossed with a rich, savory tomato and meat sauce. Quick, comforting, and full of flavor, it’s perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions. Simple ingredients, bold taste, and a touch of fresh herbs make this a classic everyone will love.

Ingredients (Serves 4-6)

  • 12 oz (340g) spaghetti noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz (680g) marinara or tomato sauce
  • 1 can (14.5 oz / 410g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil or parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef (or sausage) with onion and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in marinara sauce, crushed tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer 10–15 minutes to develop flavor.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil or parsley and sprinkled with Parmesan cheese.
