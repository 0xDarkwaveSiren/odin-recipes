Little Pockets

Description

Delicious homemade or store-bought ravioli stuffed with cheese and herbs, served with a rich tomato sauce or creamy butter sauce. Perfectly tender pasta pillows that melt in your mouth, making it an elegant yet comforting Italian dish for any occasion.

Ingredients (Serves 4-6)

  • 1 package (about 24) cheese ravioli (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz (680g) marinara or tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional, for creamy sauce)
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley, chopped, for garnish

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook ravioli according to package instructions until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add marinara sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5–10 minutes. For a creamy sauce, stir in heavy cream at this stage.
  4. Add cooked ravioli to the sauce and gently toss to coat without breaking the pasta.
  5. Serve hot, sprinkled with grated Parmesan and garnished with fresh basil or parsley.
