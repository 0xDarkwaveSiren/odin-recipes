Layers of Love

Description

A rich, hearty, and comforting Italian classic, this lasagna layers tender pasta sheets with a savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta filling, and gooey melted cheese. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, it’s the ultimate crowd-pleaser that combines bold flavors and a beautiful golden top.

Ingredients (Serves 6-8)

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz (680g) marinara or tomato sauce
  • 1 can (14.5 oz / 410g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef (or sausage) with onion and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in marinara, crushed tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg until smooth.
  6. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  7. Layer 3–4 noodles on top, then spread half of the ricotta mixture, sprinkle 1/3 of mozzarella, and add more meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with noodles topped with remaining sauce and mozzarella. Sprinkle Parmesan on top.
  8. Cover with foil (to prevent sticking, tent foil slightly) and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove foil and bake an additional 20–25 minutes, until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  10. Let stand 10–15 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
