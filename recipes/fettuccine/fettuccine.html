Cream of Ribbons

Description

Creamy, rich, and indulgent, Fettuccine Alfredo is a classic Italian pasta dish featuring tender fettuccine noodles coated in a luscious butter and Parmesan cream sauce. Perfect for a comforting dinner or a special treat, it’s simple yet elegant.

Ingredients (Serves 4)

  • 12 oz (340g) fettuccine noodles
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter. Add garlic (if using) and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and simmer for 3–5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add grated Parmesan cheese gradually, stirring until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  5. Toss the cooked fettuccine in the sauce until fully coated.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley.
